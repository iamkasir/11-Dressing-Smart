Privacy policy

At Dressing Smart, we understand and respect the importance of our customers’ privacy and the data they provide us in connection with their transactions. We want our customers to understand how we use, store, and share their information. This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected by Dressing Smart, including our website, emails, and product purchases.

We use the information we obtain from our customers to process orders and personalize products, to inform customers of our new product offerings, and to improve our customer service. This information includes, but is not limited to, names, addresses, telephone numbers, payment information, and email addresses. We do not sell or rent any customer information to third parties, and we will not share any customer information without first obtaining the customer’s express permission.

We have implemented reasonable technical, administrative, and physical measures to ensure that our customer’s information is secure. We use encryption technology, firewalls, and other safeguards to protect customer information from unauthorized access. All customer information stored on our servers is encrypted.

We may use third-party vendors to assist us with projects such as providing online customer support and fulfilling customer orders. We take steps to ensure that any third-party vendors we use adhere to our privacy policies, but we cannot guarantee their compliance with all applicable laws. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any third-party vendors.

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time, so please check back periodically to stay informed. If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us.
